Your Questions Answered

Bodysculpting FAQ

Is bodysculpting painful?
Not at all. Most clients find treatments very comfortable and relaxing. Laser lipolysis feels like a gentle warming sensation, cavitation like a warm massage, and RF like a hot stone treatment. Many clients fall asleep!
Is there any downtime after treatment?
No downtime at all. You can return to your normal daily activities straight after your appointment. You may notice mild redness or warmth in the treated area, but this settles quickly.
When will I see results?
Some clients notice changes after their very first session. The best results are seen after a full course as your body continues to process and eliminate fat naturally. Results improve progressively over time.
Is bodysculpting a weight loss treatment?
Bodysculpting is a body contouring solution, not a weight loss treatment. It targets stubborn areas that don't respond to diet and exercise, and works best alongside a healthy lifestyle.
How can I maximise my results?
Drink 2–3 litres of water daily for 48–72 hours after each session, stay active, avoid alcohol and caffeine for 24 hours before and after, and eat a balanced diet to help your body flush out released fat.
Are there any contraindications?
Treatments are not suitable if you are pregnant, have a pacemaker, metal implants in the area, uncontrolled diabetes, or certain other conditions. A full consultation is always carried out before your first session.
What should I do before my appointment?
Drink 1–2 litres of water beforehand, eat a light meal, avoid alcohol and caffeine, and skip heavy creams on the treatment area. Wear comfortable clothing that gives easy access to the area being treated.
Can I pay in instalments?
